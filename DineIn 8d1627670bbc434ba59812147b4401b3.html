Bettering Engagement through Gamification:

One way of bettering engagement is by incentivizing the user with some “Umami points” which they can later redeem as offers/discounts on food

Introducing a swiping method to indicate the user’s affinity/anti-affinity for a dish ordered previously may also foster better engagement by way of gamification.

Bettering Engagement through Gamification

One way of bettering engagement is by incentivizing the user - quantifying the user’s activity with some "Umami points" - which they can later redeem as offers/discounts on food.

Based on activity, a 10-day-streak (daily use of the app) is also introduced to provide the user with some incentive to return - a measure which fosters further user engagement.

Drawing on the design of some real estate selling apps that leveraged on the ubiquity of swipe-left-to-reject and swipe-right-to-like in dating apps;

Introducing swiping as a method to indicate the user’s affinity/anti-affinity for a dish ordered previously may also promote better engagement by way of gamification.

Creating a Community with Shared Responsibility

Based on a point drawn from User Research, there aren’t provisions for sustaining a community within existing food delivery systems.

Hence, DineIn had a provision for creating Chat Forums/Discussions clustered by:

  1. Location/Neighborhood
  1. Cuisines
  1. Restaurants

Finally, the user also has an option of initiating discussions with their friend.
